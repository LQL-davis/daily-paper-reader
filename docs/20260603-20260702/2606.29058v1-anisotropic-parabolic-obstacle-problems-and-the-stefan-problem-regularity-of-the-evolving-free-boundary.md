## 摘要
我们研究在障碍约束下，曲面按各向异性平均曲率流演化的抛物型障碍问题。给定一个凸障碍和初始数据，我们寻找一个演化曲面，在保持位于障碍之上的同时最小化各向异性能量泛函；作为特例，该框架包含各向异性Stefan问题，其中自由边界代表具有方向依赖表面张力的相变界面。核心工具是Cahn-Hoffman变换 $S(x) = A^{-1/2}x$，它将Wulff椭球 $\{x : x^T A^{-1}x \leq 1\}$ 映射到欧氏单位球，并将各向异性问题转化为一个等价的各向同性问题，并在自由边界上带有广义Robin型条件。我们证明了解的最优正则性（在空间上为 $C^{1,\alpha}$，在时间上为 $C^{0,\alpha/2}$，直至自由边界），以及在非退化点处演化自由边界的 $C^{1,\alpha}$ 正则性。时空奇异集的抛物型Hausdorff维数至多为 $n - 1$。

## Abstract
We study a parabolic obstacle problem for surfaces evolving by anisotropic mean curvature flow subject to an obstacle constraint. Given a convex obstacle and initial data, we seek an evolving surface minimizing an anisotropic energy functional while remaining above the obstacle; as a special case, this framework includes the anisotropic Stefan problem, where the free boundary represents a phase transition interface with direction-dependent surface tension. The central tool is the Cahn--Hoffman transform $S(x) = A^{-1/2}x$, which maps the Wulff ellipsoid $\{x : x^T A^{-1}x \leq 1\}$ to the Euclidean unit ball and converts the anisotropic problem into an equivalent isotropic one with a generalized Robin-type condition on the free boundary. We prove optimal regularity of the solution ($C^{1,α}$ in space and $C^{0,α/2}$ in time up to the free boundary) and $C^{1,α}$-regularity of the evolving free boundary at non-degenerate points. The parabolic Hausdorff dimension of the space-time singular set is shown to be at most $n - 1$.
