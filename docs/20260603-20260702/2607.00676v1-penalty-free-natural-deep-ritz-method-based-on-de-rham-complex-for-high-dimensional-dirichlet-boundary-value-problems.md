## 摘要
深度神经网络在高维偏微分方程中展现出巨大潜力，然而施加本质边界条件仍具挑战，尤其罚参数随维数升高需针对特定问题重新调整。本文基于de Rham复形及其免罚边界分解，将自然深度Ritz方法（NatDRM）[H. Yu and S. Zhang, J. Comput. Phys., 537 (2025)] 扩展为适用于所有维度 $d \geq 2$ 的统一框架：旋度型算子分别作用于二维中的标量势、三维中的向量势、以及 $d \geq 4$ 时的反对称二阶张量势。该方法将Dirichlet约束转化为三个耦合的自然（Neumann型）子问题及相应的Ritz型损失，从而无需边界惩罚参数 $β$。我们导出了维度统一的离散损失、基于边界的轻量级规范固定正则化以解决旋度核的非唯一性，并给出了联合训练流程；在第一个子问题层面给出了对变系数椭圆型与半线性Poisson问题的推广。在高达6维的光滑基准上的数值实验表明，NatDRM无需任何罚参数调节，在多数情形下与最优调参的罚方法DRM和PINN相比，精度持平或更高。在6维中，罚DRM在多数罚值下失效，而NatDRM实现稳定收敛，且内部与边界误差同步衰减，解决了罚方法固有的失衡问题。

## Abstract
Deep neural networks show great promise for high-dimensional PDEs, yet enforcing essential boundary conditions remains challenging, especially as penalty parameters require problem-specific retuning with increasing dimensionality. In this work, we extend the Natural Deep Ritz Method (NatDRM) [H. Yu and S. Zhang, J. Comput. Phys., 537 (2025)] to a unified framework for all dimensions $d \geq 2$ based on the de Rham complex and its penalty-free boundary decomposition: curl-type operators act on scalar potentials in 2D, vector potentials in 3D, and antisymmetric second-order tensor potentials in $d \geq 4$, respectively. This method converts Dirichlet constraints into three coupled natural (Neumann-type) subproblems with corresponding Ritz-type losses, eliminating the need for a boundary penalty parameter $β$. We derive dimension-unified discrete losses, lightweight boundary-based gauge-fixing regularizations to resolve curl-kernel non-uniqueness, and a joint training procedure; extensions to variable-coefficient elliptic and semilinear Poisson problems are formulated at the first subproblem level. Numerical experiments on smooth benchmarks up to 6D show that NatDRM, without any penalty tuning, matches or exceeds the accuracy of optimally tuned DRM and PINN in most cases. It converges stably in 6D where penalized DRM fails for most penalty values, and exhibits synchronous decay of interior and boundary errors, resolving the inherent imbalance of penalty-based methods.
