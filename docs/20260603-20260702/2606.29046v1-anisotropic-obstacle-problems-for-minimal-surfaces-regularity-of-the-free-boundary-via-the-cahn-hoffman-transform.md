## 摘要
本文研究在椭球型各向异性能量下曲面极小化的障碍问题。给定一个凸障碍物和一个边界值，我们寻求一个既位于障碍物上方又能极小化各向异性面积泛函的曲面。核心创新在于系统运用Cahn-Hoffman变换，将该各向异性问题转化为一个具有广义Robin边界条件的等价各向同性障碍问题。我们证明了在非退化条件下，解的$C^{1,1}$最优正则性（直至自由边界）以及自由边界本身的$C^{1,\alpha}$正则性。自由边界的奇异集被证明其豪斯多夫维数至多为$n-1$，同时对数型外膜不等式蕴含了其$(n-1)$-可求积性。该方法融合了Caffarelli经典的障碍问题理论、各向异性平均曲率的几何理论以及适配于各向异性情形的Alexandrov反射原理。

## Abstract
We study an obstacle problem for surfaces minimizing an anisotropic surface energy of ellipsoidal type. Given a convex obstacle and a boundary datum, we seek a surface that minimizes the anisotropic area functional while remaining above the obstacle. The central novelty is the systematic use of the Cahn-Hoffman transform to convert the anisotropic problem into an equivalent isotropic problem with a generalized Robin boundary condition. We prove optimal regularity of the solution ($C^{1,1}$ up to the free boundary) and $C^{1,α}$-regularity of the free boundary itself under a non-degeneracy condition. The singular set of the free boundary is shown to have Hausdorff dimension at most $n-1$, and a logarithmic epiperimetric inequality yields its $(n-1)$-rectifiability. The approach combines Caffarelli's classical theory of obstacle problems with the geometric theory of anisotropic mean curvature and the Alexandrov reflection principle adapted to the anisotropic setting.
