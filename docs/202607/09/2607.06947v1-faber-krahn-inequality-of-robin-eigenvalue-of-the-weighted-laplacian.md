## 摘要
本文证明了在$\mathbb{R}^{n}$和$\mathbb{H}^{n}$上带Robin边界条件的加权拉普拉斯算子第一特征值的两个Faber-Krahn型不等式。在两种情形中，最优区域均显示为以原点为中心的球。

## Abstract
In this paper, we prove two Faber-Krahn-type inequalities for the first eigenvalue of the weighted Laplacian with Robin boundary conditions on $\mathbb{R}^{n}$ and $\mathbb{H}^{n}$. In both settings, the optimal domain is shown to be a ball centered at the origin.
