## 摘要
我们研究了具有固定面积的平面闭合曲线在其弹性能量的负 \( L^2 \) 梯度下的演化。对于光滑初始数据，我们建立了流的局部与全局存在性。通过施加简单性假设和初始能量界，我们证明演化曲线的长度保持一致有界。这导致了向临界点的子收敛，随后利用 Łojasiewicz–Simon 不等式将其提升为完全收敛。相反，对能量剖面曲线的分析——它将给定长度映射到具有该长度和固定面积的所有曲线中的最小能量——揭示了对于满足特定长度和能量标准的初始数据，长度会发散到无穷大。我们通过数值模拟可视化了这些发现。

## Abstract
We study the evolution of plane closed curves with fixed area moving by the negative $L^2$-gradient of their elastic energy. For smooth initial data, we establish local and global existence of the flow. By imposing a simplicity assumption and an initial energy bound, we show that the length of the evolving curve remains uniformly bounded. This yields subconvergence to a critical point, which is then improved to full convergence by utilizing a Łojasiewicz--Simon inequality. Conversely, an analysis of the energy profile curve, which maps a given length to the minimal energy among all curves with that length and fixed area, reveals that the length diverges to infinity for initial data satisfying specific length and energy criteria. We visualize our findings through numerical simulations.
