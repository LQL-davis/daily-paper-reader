## 摘要
我们处理具有混合（等式和不等式）期望值约束的随机弱凸优化这一挑战性问题。虽然无约束弱凸问题存在最优的$\mathcal{O}(ε^{-3})$样本复杂度算法，但处理复杂的函数约束通常需要繁琐的多循环惩罚或增广拉格朗日方法，这些方法存在内循环复杂度高和参数调节敏感的问题。为了弥补这一根本差距，我们提出了原始-对偶Halpern-PAGE（PD-HP）算法。作为一种纯粹的单循环方法，PD-HP完全避免了嵌套迭代的计算负担。每一步只需解决一个简单的强凸代理子问题以及直接的对偶投影，这使得它异常高效且易于实现。关键的是，我们证明了这种计算轻量级的算法对于混合约束的随机弱凸问题达到了最优的$\mathcal{O}(ε^{-3})$样本复杂度，成功匹配了理论下界。此外，当原始域是紧多面体凸集时，我们通过利用广义Mangasarian-Fromovitz约束规范（MFCQ）以及Hoffman误差界，建立了对偶乘子的确定性稳定性。这确保了我们的最优复杂度界在标准的无界KKT残差度量下严格成立，没有任何理论差距或人为残差截断。

## Abstract
We tackle the challenging problem of stochastic weakly convex optimization subject to mixed (equality and inequality) expected-value constraints. While optimal $\mathcal{O}(ε^{-3})$ sample complexity algorithms exist for unconstrained weakly convex problems, dealing with complex functional constraints typically requires cumbersome multi-loop penalty or augmented Lagrangian methods, which suffer from high inner-loop complexity and sensitive parameter tuning. To bridge this fundamental gap, we propose the primal-dual Halpern-PAGE (PD-HP) algorithm. As a purely single-loop method, PD-HP completely bypasses the computational burden of nested iterations. At each step, it merely requires solving a simple strongly convex surrogate subproblem alongside a straightforward dual projection, making it exceptionally efficient and convenient to implement. Crucially, we prove that this computationally lightweight algorithm achieves the optimal $\mathcal{O}(ε^{-3})$ sample complexity for mixed-constrained stochastic weakly convex problems, successfully matching the theoretical lower bounds.   Furthermore, when the primal domain is a compact polyhedral convex set, we establish the deterministic stability of the dual multipliers by exploiting the generalized Mangasarian-Fromovitz constraint qualification (MFCQ) alongside Hoffman's error bound. This ensures that our optimal complexity bound holds strictly under the standard, unbounded KKT residual metric without any theoretical gaps or artificial residual truncations.

### 2. 论文提出的方法论
本文提出 **原始-对偶 Halpern-PAGE (PD-HP) 算法**，它是一项纯单循环方法。其核心思想是融合两种数学机制，精准解决上述痛点：

*   **Halpern 锚定加速**
    *   **动因**：因目标函数与约束的弱凸性，其对应的 KKT 算子（即梯度映射）仅具有弱单调性，导致原始-对偶梯度流容易发散或振荡。

*   **PAGE 方差缩减**
    *   **动因**：为突破标准随机梯度方法的 $\mathcal{O}(\epsilon^{-4})$ 复杂度瓶颈。
    *   **机制**：使用马尔可夫方差缩减技术，交替执行：低频的大批量精确梯度估计（以概率 $p$ 使用大批量 $B$）和高频的小批量递归更新（以概率 $1-p$ 使用小批量 $b$），在保证梯度估计低方差的同时实现极低的平均单步计算成本。
*   **算法流程**：每一步仅需执行两个基础运算：
    1.  **原始步 (Primal Step)**：在当前梯度估计 $v_t$ 引导下，求解一个**强凸的代理子问题** $\min_x \langle v_t, x\rangle + \frac{1}{2\alpha}\|x - x_t\|^2$。这在众多场景下存在闭式解或易于计算。
