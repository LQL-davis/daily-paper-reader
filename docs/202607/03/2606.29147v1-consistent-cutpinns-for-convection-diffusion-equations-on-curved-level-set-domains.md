## 摘要
我们给出了弯曲水平集区域上稳态对流扩散方程的相容性PINN先验误差分析。标准均方内部损失在对流主导区域失效：解产生了O(ε)的边界层，其中逐点残差增长如ε^{-1}，所以损失由边界层内少数配点主导，导致光滑内部区域未解析。我们通过以离散L^p_γ范数（γ=1+1/log(mtil)，这是H^{-1}稳定性项的可计算替代）惩罚内部残差，并通过离散H^{1/2}_{边界}迹范数施加边界条件（该范数统一处理平坦和弯曲几何），消除了这种不匹配。在Besov正则性假设下，我们证明了单个先验H^1误差界，对所有内部指数γ∈(1,2]有效，其最优恢复率由弯曲几何特有的切割单元下限1/(2γ)控制。在矩形和圆盘上，ε=2^{-s}, s∈{2,4,6}的数值实验证实了分析：随着边界层变得陡峭，L^2内部损失对初始化敏感，而L^p_γ内部则稳定训练，内部范数是收敛的决定因素。

## Abstract
We present an a priori error analysis of consistent-loss PINNs for stationary convection-diffusion equations on curved level-set domains. The standard mean-squared interior loss fails in the convection-dominated regime: the solution develops an $O(\eps)$ boundary layer in which the pointwise residual grows like $\eps^{-1}$, so the loss is dominated by the few collocation points inside the layer and leaves the smooth bulk unresolved. We remove this mismatch by penalising the interior residual in a discrete $\Lpγ$ norm with $γ= 1 + 1/\log\mtil$, a computable surrogate for the $\Hminusone$ stability term, and imposing the boundary condition through a discrete $\HhalfBdry$ trace norm, which treats flat and curved geometries uniformly. Under Besov regularity assumptions we prove a single a priori $\Hone$ error bound, valid for all interior exponents $γ\in (1,2]$, with an optimal recovery rate governed by a cut-cell floor $1/(2γ)$ specific to the curved geometry. Numerical experiments on a rectangle and a disk at $\eps = 2^{-s}$, $s \in \{2,4,6\}$, confirm the analysis: as the layer sharpens, the $\Lp{2}$ interior loss becomes seed-fragile while the $\Lpγ$ interior trains reliably, the interior norm being the decisive factor in convergence.
