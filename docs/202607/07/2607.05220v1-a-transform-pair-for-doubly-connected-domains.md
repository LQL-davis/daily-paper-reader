## 摘要
针对有界双连通域，发展了一种推广统一变换方法的新变换技术，作为全纯函数和拉普拉斯方程解的边值问题数值求解的新途径。该工作建立在Crowdy (2015, IMA J., 80) 针对多连通圆形域发展的变换方法，以及H., Lanzani, Llewellyn Smith, and Luca (2025, Proc. A, 481) 针对单连通有界域发展的方法之上。圆环的Szegö核及相应的变换法则对这一新技术的推导至关重要。通过对两个区域上的修正Schwarz问题进行了实现，以展示该新方法的有效性。

## Abstract
A new transform-based technique that generalizes the unified transform method is developed for bounded doubly connected domains as a novel way to numerically solve boundary value problems for holomorphic functions and solutions to the Laplacian. This work builds on the transform methods for multiply connected circular domains developed by Crowdy (2015, IMA J., 80) and the methods for simply connected bounded domains developed by H., Lanzani, Llewellyn Smith, and Luca (2025, Proc. A, 481). The Szegö kernel of the annulus and a corresponding transformation law is pivotal in the derivation of this new technique. The modified Schwarz problem for two domains is implemented to demonstrate the effectiveness of this new method.
